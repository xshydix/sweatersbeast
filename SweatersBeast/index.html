About

Thrift language itself is taken from the word thrive which means to develop or progress. While the words thrifty itself can be interpreted as a way to use money and other goods properly and efficiently. It can also be interpreted that thrifting is the activity of buying used goods. However, this activity is not just buying used goods, but what about personal satisfaction if you can get cool or rare items at half the price or cheaper.

SWEATERSBEAST was founded in 2019 in the midst of the thrifting phenomenon that is being discussed, but this thrifting activity is not a trend that only occurs 1-2 years, if sorted from the first time this culture appeared it might return to 1 century ago when the industrial revolution occurred.
